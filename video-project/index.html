About The Production:

The production of The Laundry Chronicles was a collaborative effort that brought a humorous and relatable story to life. Shot in a dorm laundry room and hallway, the team worked to capture the protagonist’s emotional journey through natural settings and dynamic cinematography. While challenges like tight spaces and continuity arose, teamwork and creativity made the process both rewarding and fun—resulting in a film as entertaining as the bloopers created along the way.
